Quick Trip

This is a native Android application I designed and build for my third year project. Quick Trip estimates how much a car journey will cost a user based on their particular cars MPG (Miles per Gallon), how far there are travelling and how much they are paying for petrol.

This project allowed me to strengthen my skills in Java, PHP, SQL, JSON and XML. I also learned how to use Asynchronous queries and the Google Maps API.
